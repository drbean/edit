Hsin Fu Yuan Company uses locally grown peanuts that contain no preservatives or additives and which are plump and well-rounded. Through a detailed and complex process, each peanut is ground to produce a peanut butter that is fragrant and thick, and that has a distinctive taste and texture. Hsin Fu Yuan conserves the heritage of Hakka life experiences and wisdom, and through diversification has developed a series of peanut butter products to fit in with the needs of modern life.
