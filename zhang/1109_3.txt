Using the grateful and charitable character of the Hakka people to strengthen relations among kinsfolk  as a starting point, the calm and steady manner of the Hakka people in dealing with affairs, and their polite manner in which they treat people are translated into a two-handed ritual practice, and has the meaning, while presenting somebody with a gift, you are also able to express your innermost sentiments by "respectfully offering chopsticks, while bringing you happiness as natural outcome" (pronunciation of the Chinese '愉快' (meaning 'happy chopsticks') is the same for happiness). In one aspect, this product conveys the message of gratitude that Hakka people have towards food, and in another aspect, it promotes the concept of using eco-friendly materials and tableware.
