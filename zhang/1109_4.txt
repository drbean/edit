The "MANNA Foods" brand came into being in order to continue the tradition of the celebrated rice cakes of its forefathers, and recalling with deep emotion the fact of being a member of the Hakka community with their thrifty and hard working spirit, reverence for heaven, and gratitude towards the earth. A contract farming method is adopted with local Hakka community farmers for them to carefully and strictly select Fuli rice of the highest quality, Gift packaging types using the words "fine, plow, rain, read" present the sincere Hakka feelings of appreciation, and also conveys the Hakka spirit of revering heaven and cherishing all things.
