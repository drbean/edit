With its mild climate and crystal clear water, the farmers in Puli Township in Nantou County can use eco-agricultural technology to plant and grow fresh and delicious non-toxic water bamboo shoots. Taiwan Water Bamboo Shoot Promotion Association uses the Puli shoots, which are full of nutrients, are low-calorie, and have a sweet flavor and refreshing taste when eaten raw, to produce water bamboo shoot dumplings (ban), a creative product derived from the Hakka New Year Glutinous Rice Cake. The dumplings are sweet tasting, and can be eaten with ease knowing that they will do no damage to the health of your body.
