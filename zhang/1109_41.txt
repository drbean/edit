With its mild climate and crystal clear water, the farmers in Puli Township in Nantou County use eco-agricultural technology to plant and grow fresh and delicious non-toxic water bamboo shoots. Taiwan Water Bamboo Promotion Association uses the Puli water bamboo shoots, which are full of nutrients, low-calorie, and have sweet flavor and refreshing taste when eaten raw, to produce water bamboo shoot dumplings (ban) - a creative product derived from the Hakka New Year Glutinous Rice Cake. This product is sweet tasting, and can be eaten with ease knowing that it will put no burden on the health of your body.
