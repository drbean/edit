"Heartfelt Planted Tea" has the implicit meaning of planting with wholehearted intention that does not run counter to the original purpose of tea, and with the tea leaves being newly picked in harmony with nature, the concept is conveyed that the tea leaves are hand picked in season. With the tea leaves enjoying an exceptional advantage by being planted and growing in a unique environment, and drawing on the mutual interweaving between the power of nature and the Hakka spirit, the organic black tea from Wu Cheng Village located in Yu Chi Township in Nantou County hopes that the glistening crimson color and luster, and the rich aroma of its black tea are able to ease the hectic pace of daily life and bring warmth and vitality to living.

"Heartfelt Planted" fine tea comes from tea planted and cultivated with wholeheartedness intention, which adopts pesticide-free agricultural methods for planting and growing that involves minimal intervention to allow symbiotic growth of the tea with the natural environment. Although full of hardships and difficult challenges, only by allowing the land to return to its original healthy state can you nurture a healthy tea that provides a robust flavor. "Heartfelt Planted" - evokes good spirited feelings with teas that are healthy and full of flavor, which are painstakingly grown but provide contented happiness! Share these teas with friends for them to strive to fulfill their every dream.

"Toasting guests with tea; receiving friends with tea; presenting tea as a gift; and chatting with tea" are events in the thread of life of Hakka people, as well as being an important symbolic representation of Hakka culture. Innovative tea snacks have been developed in response to new eating patterns, and includes the tea snack - "Classic Black Tea Nougat." When eating this nougat alongside drinking tea, you are able to better appreciate the cultural and creative tone and savor the taste of home of the Hakka people.

Using a cultivation method that is the most closest to nature without pesticides or insecticides, which involves agricultural management that uses minimal intervention and the deepest respect for nature to allow symbiotic growth of the tea with the natural environment. Since its beginnings, although Wan Da Natural Agricultural Methods Trading Company has gone through real hardships, the company abides by the original intention of sharing the most healthy and natural fine teas - receiving friends with tea.

Wu Cheng Village of Yu Chi Township is located in Nantou County, and due to its geographical location of 600 to 700 meters above sea level, with an average humidity is 81.2% and a year round average temperature of 25 degrees, the environment provides the most suitable habitat for growing black tea. Organic black tea from Wu Cheng Village is a healthy product nurtured by the natural environment.
