Zhen Mei Red Yeast Rice Pig's Knuckles is located in the large Hakka town of Fenglin Township in Hualien County. The preparation of its Pig's Knuckles follows the traditional culinary Hakka process but combines the rich and unique characteristics of Hakka pig knuckles with a modern touch, adding red yeast rice to the recipe, which improves the traditional salty flavor and oily taste and leaves the Hakka stewed pig's knuckles with a light and delectable taste. This makes Zhen Mei Red Yeast Rice Pig's Knuckles very suitable for consumption by health-conscious modern people.
