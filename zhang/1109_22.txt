Zhen Mei Red Yeast Rice Pig's Knuckles is located in the large Hakka town of Fenglin Township in Hualien County. The preparation of its Pig's Knuckles follows the Hakka traditional culinary skills that combines the rich and unique characteristics of Hakka cuisine of adding red yeast rice and pig's knuckles to the recipe, which improves the traditional salty flavor and oily taste, enabling the Hakka stewed pig's knuckles to have a light and delectable taste. This makes Zhen Mei Red Yeast Rice Pig's Knuckles highly suitable for consumption by health-conscious modern people.
