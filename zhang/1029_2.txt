The design is inspired by the Hakka blue shirt, symbolizing the inherited aesthetics of the life of the Hakka people. Through clean, simple, and modern lines, the logo conveys the traditional characteristics of Hakka culture, as it incorporates creativity and modern aesthetics. Pink represents blessings and luck, while blue symbolizes the thrifty Hakka spirit. Every Hakka TAIWAN product embodies a meaningful philosophy. With their heart-warming blessings, the products are imbued with the efforts and devotion of their producers.
