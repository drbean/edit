The design is inspired by Hakka blue shirt, symbolizing the inherited aesthetics of life of the Hakka people. Through clean, simple, and modern lines, the logo conveys the traditional characteristics of Hakka culture, and incorporates creativity and modern aesthetics. Pink represents blessing and luck, while blue symbolizes the thrifty Hakka spirit. Every Hakka TAIWAN product embodies a meaningful philosophy. With heart-warming blessing, the products will reveal the effort and devotion of the producers.
