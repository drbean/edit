Oriental Beauty Tea, formerly known as "Peng Feng Tea (a local dialect pronunciation meaning "Bragger's Tea)," is an amber colored tea with a smooth non-bitter taste that is imparted with a slight natural fruity flavor. Because of its distinctive flavor, this Oriental Beauty Tea is extremely popular, and is a major featured product of the Beipu tea industry. Guang Jun tea leaves in combination with the featured Tourism Factory are used to promote Hakka tea culture and allows visitors to directly experience the tea making process.
