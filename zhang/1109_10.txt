The flowering season for the wild ginger lily is long, and because the plant has a rich floral fragrance it often used as an ingredient in the dishes prepared by Hakka mothers. This product uses the juice extracted and purified from specially selected wild ginger lily and carefully selected CAS (Chinese Agricultural Standard) high quality pork, and adopts a vacuum low-temperature manual and Michelin six line baking method to produce a fresh, delicious, and non-greasy meat product. This allows consumers to sense the warmth of the Hakka mother's homely touch while sucking their fingers and savoring the aftertaste of the meat, as well as appreciate the Hakka love and care for living things and non-wasteful attitude towards life.
